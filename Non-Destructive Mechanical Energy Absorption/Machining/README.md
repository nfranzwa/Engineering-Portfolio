# Machining

Two stages of CNC milling on polycarbonate node discs.

| Stage | Folder | Approach |
|---|---|---|
| Prototyping | [`Prototyping/`](Prototyping) | Two work-coordinate-offset "simultaneous" machining — softjaws hold both a fresh blank and the first-op part at once, halving cycle time and proving the CAM + tooling before scaling. |
| High volume | [`High Volume/`](High%20Volume) | Once CAM and tooling were validated, switched to tabbed stock so a single setup produces many parts per run; tabs are removed with snips + sandpaper and deburred. |

All blanks were waterjet-cut polycarbonate. CAM in Fusion 360, machining on the shop mill.
