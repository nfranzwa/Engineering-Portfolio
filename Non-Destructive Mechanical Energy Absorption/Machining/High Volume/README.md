# High Volume — Tabbed Stock for Batch Runs

After CAM and tooling were validated on the prototyping setup, the workflow scaled by leaving small tabs holding each part to the stock. One setup now produces many parts per run; the tabs are removed with snips + sandpaper, then deburred.
